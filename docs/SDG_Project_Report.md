# 1. Selected SDG and Reason for Selection

## Selected SDG
SDG 4 – Quality Education

## What SDG 4 Focuses On

The goal of SDG 4 is to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

## Why This Issue Is Important

Many students struggle to find structured learning paths, reliable educational resources, career guidance, and interview preparation support. Information is often scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult for learners to stay focused and progress efficiently.

Providing personalized educational assistance can help students learn more effectively and make informed career decisions.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

## What is the Problem?

Students often face challenges such as:

- Lack of personalized learning guidance
- Confusion about career paths
- Difficulty finding quality learning resources
- Limited access to mentorship
- Insufficient interview preparation support

## Where Does This Problem Exist?

This problem exists globally, especially among school and college students who rely on online resources for learning.

## Who Is Affected?

- School students
- College students
- Fresh graduates
- Self-learners

## Why Is It Serious?

Without proper guidance, students may waste time following ineffective learning paths, become overwhelmed by information, and miss career opportunities.

## What Could Happen If It Is Not Solved?

- Poor learning outcomes
- Lower employability
- Lack of career clarity
- Reduced confidence during interviews

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

## Project Name

AI Career Mentor

## What the Project Is

AI Career Mentor is an AI-powered educational chatbot developed using Botpress. It provides students with personalized study plans, learning roadmaps, educational resources, career guidance, interview preparation support, and AI tutoring.

## How the System Works

The chatbot offers multiple educational modules:

1. Study Plan Generator
2. Learning Roadmaps
3. Resource Recommendations
4. Career Guidance
5. Interview Coach
6. AI Tutor

Users select a category and receive structured guidance tailored to their learning goals.

## How It Helps Solve the Problem

The chatbot acts as a virtual mentor that:

- Organizes learning paths
- Recommends educational resources
- Provides career guidance
- Helps prepare for interviews
- Answers learning-related questions

## Who Will Use It?

- Students
- Graduates
- Job seekers
- Self-learners

# 4. Project Features

## 1. Personalized Study Plans

Provides structured study plans for:

- Placements
- GATE Preparation
- AI/ML Learning
- Web Development

### Description

The chatbot generates goal-oriented study plans that help learners organize their daily and weekly learning activities effectively.

---

## 2. Learning Roadmaps

Provides step-by-step learning paths for:

- Python
- Data Science
- AI/ML
- Web Development

### Description

The roadmap module guides students through a structured sequence of topics, helping them learn skills in the correct order.

---

## 3. Resource Recommendations

### Description

The chatbot recommends useful courses, learning platforms, practice websites, tools, and project ideas to support skill development.

---

## 4. Career Guidance

### Description

Students can explore different technology career paths, understand required skills, and learn how to prepare for specific roles.

Supported career paths include:

- Software Engineer
- AI Engineer
- Data Scientist
- Web Developer

---

## 5. Interview Coach

### Description

Provides interview preparation guidance, technical topics to study, programming languages to focus on, and project preparation tips.

---

## 6. AI Tutor

### Description

An AI-powered tutor capable of answering questions related to programming, technology, artificial intelligence, career development, and learning strategies.
